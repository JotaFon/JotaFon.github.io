ABOUT ME

I am a Full Stack Developer working across front-end, back-end and mobile, turning operational needs into clear and maintainable digital products.

My current stack includes TypeScript, AdonisJS, PostgreSQL, React, Redux, Vite, React Native and Flutter.

Before programming, I worked with digital painting, a creative background that still influences how I think about interface clarity and visual details.

My first steps in development were at X-Brain, where I worked as an intern and later as a junior front-end developer on Claro's sales system. Today I build web and mobile features for a fleet management application.

Graduated in Systems Analysis and Development from Positivo University in Londrina - PR, Brazil, I also bring management experience, which gives me a unique perspective on business needs and user experience.
